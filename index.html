About the Program

Shape Your Career,
Explore Your Potential

Career Pathways is conceived and conducted by Lord Buddha Education Foundation (LBEF) , Nepal's first IT college, bringing decades of expertise and industry-aligned guidance to your learning journey.

Designed specifically for result-awaiting Grade 12 graduates, this program offers structured exposure to technology careers, practical coding skills, and professional development — all at zero cost.
